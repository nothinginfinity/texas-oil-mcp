# Example Questions

## Statewide

- How has Texas crude oil production changed over the last 24 months?
- Compare EIA statewide production with RRC-reported production.
- Which months show the largest difference between EIA and RRC figures?

## County

- Which Texas counties produced the most oil last month?
- Rank Midland, Martin, Reeves, and Loving County over the last 12 months.
- Which counties had the fastest year-over-year oil production growth?

## Operator

- Create a production profile for a given operator.
- Which operators increased production most in District 8?
- Show an operator's production by county and field.

## Lease and field

- Summarize production for a lease over the last year.
- Which fields are rising fastest in the Permian region?
- Show field-level oil vs gas mix by month.

## Data quality

- Why does EIA data not match RRC data?
- Is this production month preliminary or substantially complete?
- What source files support this answer?
